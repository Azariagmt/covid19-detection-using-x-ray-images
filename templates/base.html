Covid 19 detection
  • Azaria
It is critical to detect the positive cases as early as possible so as to prevent the further spread of this pandemic and to quickly treat affected patients. The need for auxiliary diagnostic tools has increased as there are no accurate automated toolkits available. Even with glimpses of hope for a cure countries in Africa(more than 1.2 billion) people dont know when they'll get it, leaving dark days still ahead. Covid-19 has resulted in countless numbers of lives lost, ruining multiple businesses all over the world and disrupting the global economy. Recent findings obtained using radiology imaging techniques suggest that such images contain salient information about the COVID-19 virus. Thus we are here trying to prove that using x-ray images we can show whether or not someone has covid and the fact that they will be intubated, and go to the intensive care unit to receive treatment afterwards.
